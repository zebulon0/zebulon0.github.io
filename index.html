About Me

Hi, I'm Zeb, your dedicated coach with a lifelong passion for athletics and helping people discover their strongest, healthiest selves. As a former college lacrosse player at RIT and 2-time back-to-back NCAA national champion, I know firsthand what it takes to reach peak performance and be the best.

With 9+ years of training experience and a lifetime as an athlete, I've helped hundreds of clients break through plateaus, build confidence, and achieve results they never thought possible. My championship mindset isn't about quick fixes or extreme measures—it's about building sustainable habits, understanding your unique needs, and creating a personalized roadmap to success.

Whether you're just starting your fitness journey or looking to take your training to the next level, I'm here to guide you every step of the way. Let's work together to unlock your potential and create lasting change that goes beyond just physical transformation.
