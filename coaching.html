Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about my coaching programs

What makes your coaching different? +

Unlike generic fitness apps or one-size-fits-all programs, everything I create is 100% personalized to YOU. Your workouts, nutrition, timeline, and support are all customized based on your goals, lifestyle, and preferences. Plus, you have direct access to me 24/7.

How quickly will I see results? +

Most clients notice improvements in energy and strength within the first 2 weeks. Visible physical changes typically start showing around 4-6 weeks, with significant transformations happening around the 3-month mark when you stay consistent.

How much time do I need to commit? +

Most clients train 3-4 times per week for 30-45 minutes. The beauty of personalized programming is that I design everything around YOUR schedule. Busy week? We adjust. More time available? We can do more.

Do I need a gym membership? +

Not necessarily! I can design effective programs for home workouts, gym access, or anywhere in between. During our consultation, we'll discuss your situation and I'll create a plan that works with what you have available.

What if I have injuries or limitations? +

I work with clients who have various limitations all the time. We'll discuss any concerns during your free consultation and I'll design a program that works around your specific situation. Your safety and comfort always come first.

Can I cancel or change my plan? +

Yes! While I recommend longer commitments for best results, you can manage your subscription through your Stripe customer portal. You can pause, change plans, or cancel with proper notice. I'm here to support you, not trap you.
